NBA The Hall Of Fame

Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame

The Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame is a museam located in Springfield, Massachusets. This museam is also an Hall of Fame for all of basketball, awarding those who have shown massive contributions to basketball. Although coaches, referees, and anyone else who has had a major effect on the game can be placed in the Hall of Fame, we will only be examining the player category. in order to be a player placed in the Basketball Hall of Fame, one must have been retired for three years.

Our Dataset

Our main dataset was taken off of basketball-refernce.com and contained individual player per game per season data From 1950 - 2022. For the sake of our analysis, we broke up the data in such a way that we had stats for each player's year 1 and year 2. This way, we could create a prediction algorithm based for future candidates without first seeing their lifetime achievements.

The Goal

The NBA Hall of Fame is somewhat shrouded in mystery. Although we know that Hall of Famers are chosen for good reasons, it would be nice to have objective way to see which players will have the ability to one day make it into the Hall of Fame. Along with just wanting, for the sake of knowledge, to know who the best players are or potentially will be, there is also an incentive for brands to know who the next big player in order to make the brand deals before other brands try to take over.
